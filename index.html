Sudha Murthy

Born: 1950 - Present

“Life should be great rather than long.”

Biography

Sudha Murthy is a renowned Indian author, philanthropist, and social worker. Born in 1950 in Shiggaon, Karnataka, she has contributed immensely to literature and society. With over 30 books to her name, her stories often focus on social issues, culture, and the lives of ordinary people.

As the chairperson of the Infosys Foundation, Sudha has dedicated herself to various philanthropic efforts, especially in education, healthcare, and rural development. Her simple yet profound approach to life and her commitment to helping those in need have inspired many.

Sudha Murthy's writings reflect her values and experiences, making her one of the most beloved authors in India. Through her life, she continues to teach us the importance of kindness, empathy, and resilience.

Thank you, Sudha Murthy, for your inspiration and service to humanity.
